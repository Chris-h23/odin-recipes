Egg Custard

Description

An amazingly thick, creamy, and rich dessert.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 dash vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg (Optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, sugar, salt, and vanilla together in a bowl until completely combined. Pour egg mixture into 6 custard cups; sprinkle tops with nutmeg.
  3. Place cups in a baking pan and fill pan with enough hot water to reach halfway up the sides of the custard cups
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until custards are set, about 1 hour. Cool completely
